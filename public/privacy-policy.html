Privacy Policy for Verify extensions

At Verify extensions, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how our browser extension handles your data.

Data Collection and Usage

This extension:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Only accesses information about installed extensions
  • Does not transmit any data to external servers
  • All processing is done locally in your browser
  • No cookies or local storage are used

Extension Permissions

Our extension requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Access to view installed extensions
  • Access to extension management

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes to this policy.
